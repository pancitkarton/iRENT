# iRENT

## Summary Description

A database designed to manage the rental of various electronic devices, including monitors, speakers, laptops, smartphones, projectors, gaming consoles, and other equipment. The system stores information about devices, customers, and rental transactions while tracking availability and rental status.

## Project Description

This Electronics Rental Management System is developed to manage the rental of different types of electronic devices such as monitors, speakers, laptops, smartphones, projectors, cameras, speakers, gaming consoles, and more. The system maintains detailed records for each device, including device type, brand, model, serial number, rental price, condition, and current availability status. Customer information is stored in the system, including full name, contact details, identification number, and rental history.

When a device is rented, this system creates a rental record containing the selected device, rental start date, expected return date, total rental fee, and rental status like ongoing, returned, or overdue. Devices marked as unavailable cannot be rented again until they are returned, preventing scheduling conflicts and double-booking. It will also keep a complete record of past and current rentals, which allows the staff to review device usage history and customer activity when needed.

Authorized staff will be able to add new devices, update device information, remove damaged or retired equipment, and register new customers. They can also update rental records when the equipment/devices are returned. The system will allow searching devices by type, brand, or model, and generate reports such as active rentals, overdue items, total revenue within a selected period, and frequently rented devices.

The system does not manage online payments, repair services, warranty claims, or multi-branch inventory. Its primary purpose is to maintain accurate rental records and monitor device availability. It may include maintenance tracking, reminders for overdue returns, and warranty management for rented devices in future enhancements.

## Sample Queries

> 1. Find all devices currently available for rental within a given date range
> 2. Show all rental transactions for a specific customer
> 3. List the most frequently rented devices in the past month
> 4. Display all overdue rentals and the customers responsible
> 5. Calculate total revenue generated per device type over a specific period
